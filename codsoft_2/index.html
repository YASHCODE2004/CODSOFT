About Us

Cozy Orphanage has been serving the community for over 20 years, providing a safe and nurturing environment for children. Our dedicated staff and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that every child receives the care and support they need to thrive.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every child has access to the resources and support needed to achieve their full potential. We believe in empowering children with education, love, and opportunities to build a brighter future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a secure and loving environment where children can grow, learn, and develop essential life skills. We strive to foster a sense of community and belonging among the children, helping them to feel valued and cared for.

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of passionate individuals who are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of the children we serve. From experienced social workers to dedicated volunteers, each member of our team brings unique skills and a shared commitment to our cause.
